# PRR - Communication With Flock
## Request Id:
* EPD-2025-2981

## Status
* Open - Partial Fulfillment

## Request 
1. Any contracts made between Eugene Police Department and Flock Safety. 
2. Any sales agreements between Eugene Police Department and Flock Safety. 
3. Email and text communications between any Eugene Police Department employee or representative and employees or representatives of Flock Safety from August 1, 2024 through June 27, 2025. 
4. Installation orders and permits for cameras leased, borrowed, or purchased from Flock Safety. 
5. Any grant applications submitted by Eugene Police Department for funds from Organized Retail Theft Grant Program. 
6. Progress reports submitted to the Criminal Justice Commission of Oregon for the Organized Retail Theft Grant Program or other grant used to pay Flock Safety. 
7. Detailed breakdown of the use of funds awarded under the Organized Retail Theft Grant, including recurring fees charged by Flock Safety.
